About us

Built for coaches,
by coaches.

SportAtlas started with a simple idea: coaches deserve better tools. We built the platform we always wished existed — one place for every drill, every team, every game.

Our mission

We believe great coaching changes lives. SportAtlas gives every coach — from grassroots youth clubs to elite academies — the tools to plan smarter, analyse deeper, and build better athletes.

We support 6 sports and every level from U10 to Adult, with AI-powered features that make advanced coaching accessible to everyone.

What we build

📋 Drill Library

A searchable library of drills with interactive court diagrams, video links, and coaching notes — yours to build and share.

🤖 AI Coach

An AI assistant trained on coaching best practices that generates drills, plans sessions, and answers tactical questions in seconds.

📊 Match Analysis

Live stat tracking and a public broadcast link so parents and fans can follow the game in real time.

👥 Team & Club Management

Rosters, attendance, calendars, chat, and tournament tools — everything a team or club needs in one place.
